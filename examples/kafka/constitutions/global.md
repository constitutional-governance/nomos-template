# Global Constitution

> Platform-wide principles that apply across all domains.
> Replace with your organization's actual principles.

## Naming

All resources are named to express ownership, domain, and lifecycle. Every name must be readable without context.

## Versioning

Breaking changes require a new resource version (e.g., topic `v1` → `v2`). Old versions are deprecated, not deleted.

## Access control

Least-privilege by default. Service accounts are scoped to a single system. Debug accounts are dev-only.

## Governance

Every platform decision has an ADR. ADRs are the source of truth for why things are the way they are.
